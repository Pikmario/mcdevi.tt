Please keep in mind that we tried to find the absolute best prices for each area, but it is completely up to you as to where you would like to stay. None of the above are “group rates”; none of the hotels we talked to (50+!) were willing to give group rates.

If you intend to make a mini vacation out of the weekend (as we know some of you are) and would like to spend a little more for a better room, we recommend 931 Beach Guest House, The Virginia Hotel, The Chalfonte, or the Beach Shack (pet friendly), or any of the adorable B&Bs in the area. These places start at about $200-$300/night and have very good reviews.

As for Wildwood, we will tell you that there is a Fireman's Convention going on the weekend of our wedding, so some hotels are completely booked up. The ones I listed above have available rooms.
